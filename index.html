About Me

I attended California State Monterey Bay where I studied Communications and Humanities with an Emphasis in Africana Studies. After Graduating and deciding to move on in my career I was granted the wish of playing pro baseball in the Miami Marlins oranization ... but this was short lived. After Major League Baseball disbanding 40 minor league team and covid striking during my most important season I was out of a job. I was now 23 years old in a pandemic with no real life skills a so so degree and no clue in which direction I wanted my career to go in.
